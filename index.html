Personal Accident insurance

This is an annual policy which provides compensation in the event of injuries, disability or death caused by violent accidental and visible events

Home Insurance

This commonly known as Domestic Package or Homeowners Insurance is an insurance coverage that offers you protection against loss or damage to the exterior structure and/or contents of your residence

Read More

Health Insurance

This refers to a type of general insurance, which provides financial assistance to policyholders when they are admitted to hospitals for treatment. Additionally, some plans also cover the cost of treatment undertaken at home, prior to a hospitalisation or after discharge from the same.

Read More

Business Combined Cover

This is a flexible insurance cover which protects the small and medium sized business from a wide range liabilities and risks arising from business operations

Read More

Key Person/ Key Man Insurance

In business, every employee is important but some employees are essential. Whether it’s their reputation name, skill set, clients or investors they bring into the company, the loss of such employees can have devastating impact on an organisation. This is type of insurance cover comes in to provide a critical financial cushion that help stabilise business while leadership finds a new way.

Read More

Business Property Insurance

This is an insurance cover that can provide protect your company’s physical assets from unexpected events, including fires, theft and vandalism. Physical assets covered under business property insurance could include business property you own or rent, office equipment, furniture & fixtures, stock and other items you count on to support daily operations.

Read More

Workman’s Compensation Policy

(commonly known as WIBA Insurance), is an insurance cover that protects employees of the insured whilst on duty they are injured or die in the course of their work.

Read More

Marine Insurance

An insurance that covers the loss or damage of ships, cargo, terminals and any transport by which the property is transferred, acquired or held between the point of origin and the final destination.

Read More

Directors and Officers Liability Insurance

This is also referred to D&O Insurance is an insurance cover that protects corporate directors and officers in the event that they are personally sued for actual or alleged wrong doing when managing a company

Read More

Agricultural Insurance

Agricultural Insurance is a policy which involves the insured (farmer) usually in percentage to an insurance company (insurer) to guarantee against loss due to any of the perils (death, flood, drought etc) covered for a particular period of time (usually not more than one year) with a promise to indemnify (pay back the value of loss) should such occur on livestock rearing and crop produce.

Read More

Cyber Liability Insurance

Cyber liability insurance is a type of insurance policy that protects businesses from the expenses incurred after the result of a data breach involving sensitive customer information, including stolen or damaged intellectual property
